## Flappy Bird Sound Design {#flappy-bird-sound-design}  
The sound design of *Flappy Bird* is intentionally minimal yet effective, focusing on creating an immersive and addictive gameplay experience.  

1. **Flap Sound**: A short, crisp "flap" sound effect is triggered each time the player taps the screen to make the bird flap its wings. This provides immediate auditory feedback and reinforces the player's actions.  
2. **Background Music**: An upbeat, looping melody with a retro synth style sets the game's energetic tone. The music is designed to be catchy and persistent, encouraging continuous play.  
3. **Crash Sound**: A brief "crash" or "explosion" sound plays when the bird collides with a pipe, signaling failure and adding a sense of urgency.  

The sounds are carefully balanced to maintain simplicity while enhancing the game's challenge and rhythm.
